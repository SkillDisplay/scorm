Roles in TYPO3

Description

In content management systems, roles and rights are differentiated for backend users, to best support their tasks and hide unnecessary complexity. In TYPO3 there is always at least one administrator user who takes care of roles and rights of backend users and other issues.

Goals

  • I know the roles in TYPO3 and their corresponding tasks.
  • I know the tasks assigned to me according to my role.
  • I know whom to ask for issues that are not part of my role.
